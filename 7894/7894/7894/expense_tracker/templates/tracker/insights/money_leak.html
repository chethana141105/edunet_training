What are Money Leaks?

Money leaks are small, frequent expenses that don't seem significant individually but add up to substantial amounts. For example, buying coffee every day ($5 × 30 days = $150/month) or impulse purchases at convenience stores. Our detector identifies categories where you have transactions below your set leak threshold. Even a single small transaction is flagged so you can stay aware of potential leaks.
